About Jai Shree Cashew

Our Story

Processing Excellence Since 2016

Jai Shree Cashew Industries was founded in 2016 at Kaladera, Jaipur with a singular mission — to bring the finest quality cashew kernels to India's retail and B2B market.

We import raw cashews from West Africa and Vietnam — the world's finest cashew-growing regions — and process them at our state-of-the-art FSSAI-certified facility.

Our team of expert processors ensures that every kernel meets international grading standards before it reaches your table.
