Our Story

Bringing Farm-Fresh
Cashews to India

Based in Kaladera, Rajasthan — Jai Shree Cashew Industries has spent nearly a decade perfecting the art of premium cashew procurement and processing. Every kernel is a promise of crispiness, taste, and health.

We source raw cashew nuts (RCN) directly from West Africa (Nigeria, Ghana, Ivory Coast, Togo, Benin), North Africa (Guinea Bissau, Senegal, Gambia) and East Africa (Tanzania) — ensuring year-round supply of the finest quality.

Direct Sourcing from 8+ African Countries
No middlemen — direct relationships with farmers and exporters across West, North, and East Africa for the best price and quality.
50+ Premium Kernel Grades Available
From giant W180 whole whites to splits, scorched pieces, and butter — we stock every grade your business needs.
