Inside Our World

From Farm to Factory

Every kernel we ship begins its journey thousands of kilometres away — from West Africa and Vietnam — and ends in precision-graded, vacuum-packed export-ready form at our Kaladera facility.

STEP 01
RCN Sourcing
RAW CASHEW NUT PROCUREMENT

Direct from Source.
West Africa & Vietnam.

We procure Raw Cashew Nuts (RCN) directly from trusted farms in Ivory Coast, Ghana, and Vietnam — ensuring KOR (Kernel Out-turn Ratio) of 46 to 50 lbs. Only the highest-grade RCN enters our processing unit.

✓ Ivory Coast · Ghana · Vietnam
✓ KOR 46–50 quality standard
✓ Direct procurement — no middlemen
STEP 02 & 03

Steam Treatment & Precision Shelling

RCN undergoes precise steam processing at controlled temperature — softening the shell for clean kernel extraction. Our shelling team uses specialist cutters to ensure maximum whole kernel integrity with minimal breakage.

✓ Controlled steam at optimal temperature
✓ Maximum whole kernel yield
✓ Trained shelling & peeling team

Return & Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2025  ·  Jai Shree Cashew Industries

1. Our Commitment to Quality

At Jai Shree Cashew Industries, every batch is quality-checked before dispatch. We take pride in our FSSAI-certified processing and vacuum-packed freshness guarantee. If you receive a product that does not meet our quality standards, we are committed to making it right.

2. Return Eligibility

Returns are accepted under the following conditions:

  • Damaged packaging on arrival: Report within 48 hours of delivery with photographic evidence
  • Wrong product dispatched: Report within 48 hours with unboxing photos/video
  • Quality defect: Significant quality deviation (mold, infestation, foreign matter) reported within 48 hours of receipt
  • Weight shortage: Documented net weight significantly below stated quantity (within 2% tolerance is industry standard)

Returns are not accepted for:

  • Change of mind after delivery
  • Minor natural variations in kernel color, size, or texture within grade specifications
  • Opened or partially consumed products (except for genuine quality defects)
  • Products that have not been stored as per instructions (cool, dry place, away from moisture)

3. Return Process

To initiate a return:

  1. Contact us within 48 hours of delivery via WhatsApp (+91 75685 77968) or email (info@jaishreecashew.com)
  2. Share your order ID, clear photographs of the product and packaging, and a description of the issue
  3. Our team will review and respond within 24 business hours
  4. Approved returns must be shipped back in original, sealed packaging. Return shipping costs are borne by us for verified defects/errors

4. Refunds

Upon receiving and inspecting the returned product:

  • Full refund: Issued for wrong product dispatched or significant quality defect
  • Partial refund or replacement: At our discretion for weight shortage or partial quality issues
  • Replacement dispatch: Offered as an alternative to refund where stock is available

Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to the original payment method. Razorpay processing times may apply (typically 3–5 additional business days for card refunds).

5. Bulk and Export Orders

For wholesale, bulk, and export orders (above 5 kg or custom B2B agreements), return and refund terms are governed by the individual purchase agreement or proforma invoice. Please contact us directly for bulk order dispute resolution.
